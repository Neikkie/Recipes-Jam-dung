How to Use Jam-dung

Jam-dung is designed to make it easy to discover authentic Jamaican recipes, browse categories, save favourites, and follow clear cooking instructions.

Getting started

1
Open the Recipes tab

The Recipes tab is where you can explore the app’s recipe collection. Recipes are grouped into meal-time categories such as Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

2
Use search to find a dish

Use the search field to look up recipes by name. This is useful when you already know what meal you want to make.

3
Filter by category

Tap the category chips to narrow results to Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner. This helps you find ideas faster based on the kind of meal you want.

4
Open a recipe card

Tap any recipe to see its detail page, including the dish image, ingredient list, measurements, and step-by-step instructions.

5
Save your favourites

Tap the heart icon on a recipe to add it to your Favourites tab. You can use this to keep your go-to dishes easy to find later.

What you can expect

  • Recipe names and images presented in a clean, scrollable layout.
  • Ingredient and measurement lists for supported recipes.
  • Instructions displayed as readable cooking steps.
  • Saved favourites stored on your device for quick access.
  • Recipe content may come from bundled app data, with some information sourced from TheMealDB.
Helpful tip:

If you run into an issue, email support.chaniiapps@gmail.com and include your device model, iOS version, a description of the problem, and screenshots if possible.
