Authentic Jamaican recipes in one simple app.

Jam-dung is a recipe app focused on authentic Jamaican dishes. Browse recipe collections, search for meals, explore breakfast, lunch, and dinner categories, save favourites, and view ingredients and step-by-step cooking instructions in a clean, easy-to-use experience.

What the app does

Browse Jamaican dishes

Jam-dung presents a collection of Jamaican recipes and organizes them into meal-time categories so it is easy to discover new dishes for different times of day.

See the details that matter

Each recipe can include a featured image, ingredient list with measurements, and cooking instructions displayed in a simple step-by-step format.

Keep favourites nearby

Users can save favourite recipes directly in the app for quicker access later without having to search again.
