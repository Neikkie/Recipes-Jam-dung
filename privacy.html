Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Jam-dung handles information when you use the app. Jam-dung is an authentic Jamaican recipes app that lets users browse recipes, search meals, view ingredients and instructions, and save favourite dishes.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Jam-dung stores your saved favourite recipes locally on your device so they can be shown again inside the app. This local storage is used to support the favourites feature.

3. Personal Information

Jam-dung does not require you to create an account to use the core app experience. We do not state that we collect personal information directly through in-app account registration, because the current app experience does not include user account creation.

4. Recipe Data

Recipe information displayed in Jam-dung may come from data bundled with the app and may also rely on third-party recipe data sources, including TheMealDB, when needed.

5. Network Access

The app may access network resources when retrieving recipe information that is not already available from bundled local data. Third-party services may process technical request data such as IP address or device networking details as part of normal internet communication.

6. Analytics and Tracking

Based on the current app structure reviewed for this page set, Jam-dung does not describe a built-in user analytics dashboard, ad SDK, or account-based tracking flow. If those features are added later, this Privacy Policy should be updated before release.

7. Data Sharing

We do not state that Jam-dung sells your personal information. Any third-party service involved in serving recipe content operates under its own terms and privacy practices.

8. Data Retention

Locally saved favourites remain on your device until you remove them in the app, clear app data, or uninstall the app, subject to how iOS manages local app storage.

9. Children’s Privacy

Jam-dung is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been provided in error, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The version posted on this page will reflect the most recent update date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support.chaniiapps@gmail.com.
